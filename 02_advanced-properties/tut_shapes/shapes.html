Triangle

Triangles or arrows are normally made with pseudo-element :after or :before tags, but Macaw doesn't handle it. I'm showing you another way to do it, not perfect but it will do the job.

Triangle is a combination of one or two transparent border with a one opaque.

/!\ Macaw don't translate “transparent” borders, so i use a rgba colors with a minimum alpha rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.01). It's only a temporary solution, don't forget to correct those values after publishing.
